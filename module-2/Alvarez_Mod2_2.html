Chapter 1: The One Where They Learn HTML

My name is Loren. I was born and raised in Puerto Rico. I currently live in Florida where I work at The Happiest Place on Earth.

If you would have asked me 10 years ago if I saw myself working at Disney, I would have said "No, I'm going to be a Doctor". And that I did. But life is a mystery, and as it turns out, even after graduating medical school you still need a lot of money and a lot of studying left to do.

For lack of the former, I could not go on to do the latter. But on a sunny November day when I was feeling stuck in life, I took to Reddit and stumbled upon Harvard's CS50 course. I felt like learning programming could be a hobby to help me cope, but it became clear quickly that I needed a more structured approach as it was progressively becoming an interesting area to me.

All this led me to where we are right now: learning HTML and finding my way into a new career. Though apprehensively, I do look forward to the future.
